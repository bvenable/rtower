Notes

  • Rolling origins and powers: a 12 can only result in two (no infinite rerolls).
  • Themes: characters with two origins use their first rolled origin for choosing a theme.
  • Energy types: usually assigned randomly from Air, Cosmic, Darkforce, Earth, Electric, Fire/Heat, Gravity, Ice/Cold, Kinetic, Light, Magnetic, Sonic, Technology, Weather, or Water
  • Mystic and psychic elemental controls have limited energy lists.
  • Gear limit: can be swapped with something else apprpriate.
  • Specialties: suggested specialties have a 50/50 chance of being taken.
